Italy v Wales

Wales did lose but both teams made it through. Italy were the threatening side despite making major rotations since they were already through. Finally a low freekick from Verrati was edged inside by Pessina. Chiesa was super super threatening in thw whole game. Later, a straight red was given to Ampadu for something that was not a high challenge. Wales were done dirty by this since it was a yellow at best. They still managed to hold on thanks to Ward’s keeping. Wales could’ve equalised if Bale who was in space kept his shot down on the volley. Italy could go semis is my prediction. Wales R16 at best.

Switzerland v Turkey

A game of great goals all round. It started off with a beauty from Seferovic. Then Shaqiri made it 2 with a beautiful weak foot shot. Then Turkey pulled one back after Sommer denied them the whole match till that point. The goal was unexpected but a banger. Then finally Switzerland sealed it from Shaqiri who was picked out by Zuber brilliantly. Their fate will be decided by the other 3rd best teams. Until then, WELL DONE BAKU.
