Day 5

Finland v Russia

Russia Domination overall. Finland did get an early chance with Pohjanpalo scoring but it was ruled out due to offside. Finally before halftime, Miranchuk scored a brilliant goal past Hradecky to get the winner. After that, a brilliant performance from Hradecky kept the score at 1-0.

Turkey v Wales

Turkey were incredibly wasteful. Ramsey put Wales in front before half time. Turkey had a few good chances but the best one was put over by Yilmaz when he was right before the goal. The final goal was from a corner and you could tell Turkey gave up. This means Wales have a chance, and Turkey are mostly out now.
