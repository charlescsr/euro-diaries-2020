Day 6

Italy v Switzerland

Italy are through with yet another 3-0 win in a dominant showing. Locatelli becoming the first Italian since Balotelli to score a brace in the Euros which capped off a great performance from the youngster. Immobile capped it off with a brilliant goal as well

Ukraine v North Macedonia

Ukraine did well in this game dominating and scoring 2 great goals. Second half was fairly even with both sides getting chances but denied by their respective keepers. A penalty later was scored but it was on the rebound from North Macedonia

Denmark v Belgium

This was a great game and a brilliant comeback from Belgium. Poulsen scored what is the second quickest goal in the Euros to a loud home crowd. It took Belgium all the way to the second half to equalise. Denmark had a lot of half chances but most of them were wasted. It was a valiant effort. The introductions of De Bruyne and Eden Hazard completely changed the game. Maybe start them next time. KDB assisting Thorgan Hazard after a brilliant run from Lukaku. After another run from Lukaku, the pass was laid off for KDB who finished brilliantly with his weak foot
