Netherlands v Ukraine

What a thriller for the Dutch on their return to a major tounrament. First Half was all square. The second half saw a few openings and Wijnaldum scored first capitalising on the rebound from the initial save. Then, Weghorst scored although may be a foul but it was given anyway. Then the comeback happened first via a SCREAMER from Yarmolenko in the top bin and then with a header from a set piece(Maybe could be the Dutch’s undoing in this tourney). Finally a pitched header from Dumfries won the game for the Dutch. Ukraine can surpirse you maybe. What a way to score your first international goal though(Dumfries).

Scotland v Czech Republic

WE HAVE FOUND THE GOAL OF THE TOURNAMENT ALREADY. What a performance from the Czechs. Patrick Schick on fire with a brace and could’ve gone for a hattrick. The second goal being an absolute belter from around 51 yards, the longest goal since around 1980. Overrall great performance.

Poland v Slovakia

Unfortunately, Krychowiak getting sent off definitely made things more harder for Poland. The winner came from Skrniar who had a brilliant first touch before striking it home.
