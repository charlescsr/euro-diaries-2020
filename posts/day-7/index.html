Day 7

Netherlands v Austria

The Dutch are through. This could have been more convincing with maybe 3 or 4 but it does the job. A penalty from Depay gave the lead and he should’ve made it 2 with an easy chance given to him. At the end, another goal from Dumfries gave them the confirmation. Austria never really had all too many chances to talk about.

Sweden v Slovakia

This was a fairly even game with chances on both sides but the keepers did well until Dubravka conceded the penalty that gave the Swedes the lead. This means the group seems finely poised since Spain drew the game against them(You have Morata to thank for that)

Croatia v Czech Republic

The next game for Croatia is a MUST WIN due to their loss in the first game. Schick scored a penalty after a VAR Review from a corner where Lovren’s stray elbow hit Schick which left him bloodied. This makes Schick the top scorer currently. Perisic equalised with a brilliant goal right after half time(What is it with immediate second half goals??). A free kick was taken quickly and Perisic cut inside before scoring.
