Day 4

Hungary v Portugal

I HAVE NO PITY TOWARDS HUNGARY. God, this game was hard to watch. Portugal were frustrated heavily by Hungary who were ultra defensive(Can give Jose a run for his money) and Jota can’t even play properly as he missed a golden chance to give the ball to CR7 and took on the shot himself. I swear the game was better when Andre Silva came on. Renato Sanches as well was a star turn as they were a breath of fresh air and capitalised on a tiring Hungary. The first came from Guerreiro which took a massive deflection. Then a penalty from CR7 made him the first person to score in 5 European Competitions. The third goal was truly a chef’s kiss. A brilliant one-two with Rafa Silva and Ronaldo rounded the keeper before finishing the goal off. Hungary did get a goal before all this carnage but it was disallowed for offside which was hilarious.
